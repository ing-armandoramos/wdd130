Rafting Site Plan

Armando Ramos

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the site is to invite everyone to experience the great outdoors by having rafting activities, we should entice customers to the adventure, team building, family gathering, and other fun activities related to the rafting world

Audience

We want everyone to experience the great outdoors and have a great rafting experience. Who are the target customers? Middle aged men with disposable income and nuclear families, another target are groups of young single adults with sufficient time to spare.What do they want? The target customers want to create memories for their families/friends in a safe and controled environment (safety is a must). This cusomers are interested in the environment and want to experience it first hand. What does the website offer? At first glance the prospect customers will see the variety of activities that they can do in the rafting club, they should see photos that can be taken with their friends, they should be able to see available dates and pricing and be able to make reservations. They should also be able to read testimonials from previous customers.
